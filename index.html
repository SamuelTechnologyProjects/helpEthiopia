ETHIOPIA HUMANITARIAN CRISIS

930,000

There are more than 930,000 refugees and asylum seekers residing in Ethiopia. A majority of the displaced people originate from South Sudan, Somalia and Eritrea (as of July 2023).

9 Million

An estimated 9 million people across Ethiopia's Tigray, Afar and Amhara regions need food aid. Nearly 40% of people in the Tigray region are suffering from an extreme lack of food.

3.1 Million

3.1 million people are internally displaced in Ethiopia (as of May 2023)

Our Commitment to Helping Refugees and Local Communities in Ethiopia

At Help Ethiopia, we are dedicated to making a meaningful impact in the lives of children and families in Ethiopia. Our mission extends to supporting local refugee camps and public schools, and our team is actively involved in various initiatives to provide aid and create positive change.

Supporting Public Schools

We believe in the power of education and its role in shaping a better future. Our efforts include assisting public schools in Ethiopia by providing essential resources and support to students and teachers.
